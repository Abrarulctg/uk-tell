ABOUT US

UK Tell Ltd is a mobile virtual network operator (MVNO) which is operating under the EE network coverage. With the simple, convenient and most accessible monthly and annual plans, we are offering our users an affordable option to choose. We have been offering a diverse range of communication services to residential users, small and medium enterprises such as teleph-ony services, SIM Only Mobile plans and various business net-working solutions. We offer international call packages at the most affordable rates.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Launched to meet a growing need for flexibility in the telecommunications setior, UK Tell is focused upon providing the best experience for our customers, always adhering to our core values.

Honesty: Building trust and honest relationships with all of our stakeholders in an open communications system.

Integrity: We base our services on the quality and the integrity which comes with the satisfaction of the customers.

Respect: We respect the privacy and the integrity of our customers and our employees.
